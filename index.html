About This Website

Have you ever wanted to know more about the world of The Dark Crystal? Then you have found the right website! The Dark Crystal has a rich and intresting history, not only in the fictional world of Thra but in our world too. This website will show you about its history and charcaters as well as some "behind the scense" of both the movie and the Netflix show.

  • Explore the History tab and see the timeline of events and what took place at what time.
  • If you want to know more about specific characters click the Characters tab to find out more about your favorite character.
  • Want to know how the The Dark Crystal was made? Go to our Behind-the-Scenes tab and see just how everything was done and created.
  • Also, you can see what's new with The Dark Crystal world by clicking on the News tab and discover the latest information on the The Dark Crystal franchise.
