Skeksis

"The Skeksis with their hard, twisted bodies, their harsh, twisted wills. For a thousand years they have ruled. - Yet now there are only ten."
- Narrator

The Skeksis are quite an intresting species that is for sure. They are the embodement of everything evil and vile in this world. When the Skeksis first appeared there were 18 of them. As time passes eight of them die leaving only ten when the movie The Dark Crystal happens.
